# Phishing Awareness & Prevention Guide

## Common Phishing Techniques
- Email spoofing
- Urgency and fear-based messages
- Fake websites and links
- Impersonation of trusted brands

## Phishing Detection Checklist
- Verify sender email address
- Avoid urgent or threatening messages
- Do not click unknown links
- Never share passwords or OTPs

## Safe vs Phishing Email Comparison

| Feature | Safe Email | Phishing Email |
|------|-----------|----------------|
| Sender | Official domain | Spoofed domain |
| Tone | Informative | Urgent |
| Links | Official URLs | Fake links |
| Attachments | Expected | Unexpected |

## Business Impact of Phishing
Phishing attacks can lead to financial loss, data breaches, reputational damage, and legal consequences for organizations.
